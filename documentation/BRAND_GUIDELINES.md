## 🌟 Brand Story

### The Origin Story
Abhyatus was born from the ancient Sanskrit concept of **abhyāsa** (अभ्यास)—the transformative power of consistent practice. In the Bhagavad Gita, Lord Krishna teaches Arjuna that through practice and detachment, the mind can be controlled and mastery achieved.

### The Modern Application
Today, we apply this timeless wisdom to help people build the habits that matter most. Whether it's meditation, exercise, learning, or personal growth, Abhyatus guides users on their journey of disciplined practice.

### The Promise
We promise to be your companion in the journey of self-transformation, providing the tools, wisdom, and support needed to achieve your highest potential through the power of consistent practice.

## 📱 User Experience Principles

### 1. **Wisdom-First Design**
Every interaction should feel meaningful and purposeful, not just functional.

### 2. **Progressive Disclosure**
Guide users through their journey without overwhelming them.

### 3. **Celebration of Progress**
Acknowledge every step forward, no matter how small.

### 4. **Cultural Authenticity**
Honor the Sanskrit roots while making them accessible to all.

### 5. **Mindful Interactions**
Design for presence and awareness, not mindless consumption.
